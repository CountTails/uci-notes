# Objectos directos

## Recognizing

- Ask the question
    - whom?
    - what?
    - which?
- Brianna come *cereal*
- *Lo* veo

## Pronombres de objectos directos

- Go in front of the verb except when verb is in the infinitive form

| Subject | Pronoun |
|:-------:|:----:|
| Yo | Me |
| Tú | Te |
| Él/Ella/Ud. | Lo/La |
| Nosotros/as | Nos |
| Vosotros/as | Os |
| Ellos/Ellas/Uds. | Los/Las |
