# Verb conjugation

## Verb structure

- Always 3 groups of verbs, dictated based on their ending in the infinitive form
    - -ar verbs
    - -er verbs
    - -ir verbs
- Consists of two parts
    - A stem (usually does not change)
    - An ending (changes based on the infinitive class)

## Conjugating -ar verbs

| Subject | Ending |
|:-------:|:------:|
| Yo | -o |
| Tú | -as |
| Él/Ella/Ud. | -a |
| Nosotros/as | -amos |
| Vosotros/as | -áis |
| Ellos/Ellas/Uds. | -an |
