# Subject pronouns

## What is a subject pronoun?

- Subject: person or thing going an action
- Pronoun: word that replaces a noun

## Singular

| Spanish | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Yo | I |
| Tú | You (informal) |
| Él/Ella/Usted(Ud.) | He/She/You (formal) |

## Plural

| Spanish | English |
|:-------:|:-------:|
| Nosotros/as | We |
| Vosotros/as | Others |
| Ellos/Ellas/Ustedes | They/You all (formal) |
